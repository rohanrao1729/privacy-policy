Privacy Policy for Thinko

Effective Date: March 16, 2025

QOTO TECHNOLOGIES PRIVATE LIMITED ("we," "our," or "us"), the creator of Thinko, respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our educational application.

This policy applies to the Thinko application and all related educational services offered by QOTO TECHNOLOGIES PRIVATE LIMITED.

1. Introduction

By using Thinko, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy. Our application is designed for educational purposes and may be used by students of all ages in schools and educational settings.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, school affiliation, grade level, and profile information.
  • Educational Data: Information about academic progress, assignments completed, and learning activities.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type, and IP address.
  • Usage Information: How you interact with the app, which features you use, and time spent on different activities.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our educational services
  • Personalize learning experiences based on student progress
  • Help teachers track student achievement and engagement
  • Analyze patterns to improve the app's educational effectiveness
  • Ensure the security and proper functioning of the application
  • Communicate important updates about the service

4. Data Security

We take security seriously and implement appropriate measures to protect your information:

  • All data is transmitted using secure HTTPS encryption
  • We implement access controls and authentication procedures
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Employee training on data privacy and security

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Schools and Teachers: To help them monitor student progress
  • Service Providers: Third parties who help us provide and improve our services (e.g., cloud storage, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by applicable law or governmental request

6. Student Privacy Protection

We are committed to protecting student privacy:

  • We respect the privacy of children, and do not collect any more personal information than reasonably necessary to enable them to participate in educational activities
  • From children under 13 years of age, we only collect limited personal information such as email address, first and last name
  • Student data is used only for intended educational functions
  • We implement strict data security and privacy controls
  • We provide transparency about data collection practices

7. Parental and School Rights

Parents, legal guardians, and school administrators have the right to:

  • Review the personal information we have collected about a student
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of a student's personal information
  • Opt out of future collection or use of the student's information

To exercise these rights, please contact us at: thinkotechsup@gmail.com

8. Data Retention

We retain student personal information only as long as needed to provide our educational services or as required by law. Upon request from a school, parent, or legal guardian, we will delete student information from our active systems.

Upon termination or expiration of your usage of Thinko, your data will be deleted from our systems within 6 months from the date of such termination or expiration. Once deleted, data cannot be recovered.

9. Mobile App Analytics

Our mobile application may use analytics tools to collect anonymized data about how you use the app. This may include information about which features you use, how long you spend in the app, and basic device information.

These analytics help us improve the user experience, fix technical issues, and understand how students and teachers interact with our educational features.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the updated policy in our application and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.

Your continued use of Thinko after any changes indicates your acceptance of the revised policy.
